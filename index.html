About Us

We are a play club for toddlers. Toddlers come from all around to play and get into trouble at our club. They play, learn, create and socialize with other toddlers. Sometimes they get into some trouble too...

Our Location

You can find us in the South East Calgary community of Copperfield. Located walking distance from parks and green spaces for the toddlers to play in when the weather permits.
